USER JOURNEY:


I. AUTHENTICATION:
    As a user, I can register for a new account
    As a user, I can sign in to my account. 
    As a user, I can log out of my account. 
    As a user, I can destroy my account. 
    As a user, I can update my password and receive an email notice.

II. USER PROFILE:
    As a user, I can update my profile information

III. ADVENTURES:
	As a user, I can add new adventures:
       -Basic info
       -Photos
       -Story
    As a user, I can upvote ADVENTURES
    As a guest, I can visit an adventure and see: 
        -basic info, 
        -photos,
        -percent of upvotes to views,
